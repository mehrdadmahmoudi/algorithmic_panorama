The Algorithmic Panorama

An Interdisciplinary Inquiry into Artificial Intelligence, Agency, and the Human Future

By Mehrdad Mahmoudi

About the Book

The Algorithmic Panorama explores the evolving relationship between artificial intelligence, human agency, technological systems, and the future of civilization. Combining insights from philosophy, cognitive science, systems theory, ethics, political thought, and technology studies, the book examines how algorithmic structures increasingly shape perception, decision-making, culture, governance, and everyday life.

Artificial intelligence is no longer merely a technological achievement. It has become a civilizational force capable of transforming economies, institutions, identities, and the future trajectory of humanity itself.

About the Author

Mehrdad Mahmoudi's academic and professional background spans physics, electrical engineering, marine technology, teaching, research, and systems analysis. His interdisciplinary approach provides the foundation for the broad intellectual scope of this book.

His current work focuses on artificial intelligence, consciousness, agency, existential risk, superintelligence, and the future of civilization.
